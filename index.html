Our History

Wakulima 1 on 1 is a Non-Govermental Organiztion(NGO) founded in 2018. The organiztion's aim was to enable farmers to market and distribute their products to the consumers without being exploited by middle-men. Formerly known as The Food Transparency Simulator, Wakulima 1 on 1 allows consumers to get value for their money. The websites shows all costs incurred by the farmers from planting, to fertilizers and even transportaion.

Our Signature Recipe

Our Signature Vegetable Salad is made with ingredients sourced fresh from our farmers. We have spents a couple of months perfecting this recipe and we hope you love it as much as we do


  1. Step One

    You will require the following ingedients:

    1. Two ripe avocadoes
    2. Some green peas
    3. Fresh corn
    4. Three onions
    5. Fresh cabbage or lettuce
    6. Yellow, Green and Red bell peppers
    7. Minced meat (optional)
    8. Salt, Pepper, Oregano, Cumin
    9. lemon or lime juice/vinegar
    10. Salad dressing

  2. Step Two

    Assemble all you ingredients and do as follows:

    1. Boil your corn and green peas
    2. Dice your onions, bell peppers and avocadoes
    3. Boil you minced meat and season to your taste. You can choose to fry it if you like it as so.
    4. Cut your lettuce or white and red cabbage into thin strips

  3. Step Three

    Toss all your prepped ingredients into a salad bowl and toss or use a salad mixer to incorporate the spices in the food and to have a cruncy taste.

  4. Step Four

    Top with some lime juice or vinegar. Voila! Enjoy your meal. Bon apppetite!
